## Stigmergy & Swarm Intelligence

- Hunt, E.R., Jones, S., Hauert, S. (2019). "Testing the limits of pheromone stigmergy in high-density robot swarms." Royal Society Open Science. https://doi.org/10.1098/rsos.190225
  — Performance peaks N≈144, degrades N≈256+, ~3% occupancy threshold

- Sayed, O., Fouad, M., Mansour, T. (2023). "Bio-Inspired Digital Pheromone Architectures for Decentralized Coordination in Dense IoT Swarm Deployments."
  — Digital pheromone stability via spectral properties, quantitative density degradation

- Heylighen, F. (2016). "Stigmergy as a universal coordination mechanism I: Definition and components." Cognitive Systems Research. https://doi.org/10.1016/j.cogsys.2015.12.002
  — Open-source development as stigmergic system, markers guide work

- Heylighen, F. (2016). "Stigmergy as a universal coordination mechanism II: Varieties and evolution." Cognitive Systems Research. https://doi.org/10.1016/j.cogsys.2015.12.007
  — Thermodynamic decay of traces, dissipation under second law

- Weyns, D., Omicini, A., Odell, J. (2007). "Environment as a first class abstraction in multiagent systems." Autonomous Agents and Multi-Agent Systems. https://doi.org/10.1007/s10458-006-0012-0
  — Digital pheromones, environment as coordination medium

- Parunak, H.V., Brueckner, S.A., Matthews, R., Sauter, J. (2005). "Pheromone learning for self-organizing agents." IEEE Trans. Systems, Man, and Cybernetics Part A. https://doi.org/10.1109/tsmca.2005.846408

- Susnea, I. (2015). "Engineering Human Stigmergy." Int. J. Comput. Commun. Control. https://doi.org/10.15837/ijccc.2015.3.87

## Reverse Annealing & Optimization

- Venturelli, D., Kondratyev, A. (2019). "Reverse quantum annealing approach to portfolio optimization problems." Quantum Machine Intelligence. https://doi.org/10.1007/s42484-019-00001-w
  — Reverse annealing >100× faster than forward on QUBO instances

- Grant, E., Humble, T.S., Stump, B. (2021). "Benchmarking Quantum Annealing Controls with Portfolio Optimization." Physical Review Applied. https://doi.org/10.1103/physrevapplied.15.014012
  — Forward vs reverse annealing experimental controls

- Ikeda, K., Nakamura, Y., Humble, T.S. (2019). "Application of Quantum Annealing to Nurse Scheduling Problem." Scientific Reports. https://doi.org/10.48550/arxiv.1904.12139
  — Reverse annealing sensitive to seeding quality, can hurt with poor seeds

- Kirkpatrick, S., Gelatt, C.D., Vecchi, M.P. (1983). "Optimization by Simulated Annealing." Science. https://doi.org/10.1126/science.220.4598.671
  — Foundational simulated annealing, monotonic cooling

- Henderson, D., Jacobson, S.H., Johnson, A.W. (2003). "The Theory and Practice of Simulated Annealing." Intl Series in OR/MS. https://doi.org/10.1007/0-306-48056-5_10
  — SA schedule variants, convergence guarantees

## Mean-Field Games

- Lasry, J.-M., Lions, P.-L. (2007). "Mean field games." Japanese Journal of Mathematics. https://doi.org/10.1007/s11537-007-0657-8
  — Canonical MFG: coupled HJB + Fokker-Planck, monotonicity for uniqueness

- Mészáros, A.R. (2015). "Density constraints in optimal transport, PDEs and mean field games." Thesis.
  — Standard MFG derivations, Lasry-Lions monotonicity, first-order difficulties

- Grover, P., Huo, M. (2024). "Phase transition in a kinetic mean-field game model of inertial self-propelled agents." Chaos. https://doi.org/10.48550/arxiv.2407.18400
  — Phase transitions, travelling-wave instabilities when monotonicity violated

- Huang, M., Malhamé, R.P., Caines, P.E. (2006). "Large population stochastic dynamic games." — Multiclass agents, Nash Certainty Equivalence
